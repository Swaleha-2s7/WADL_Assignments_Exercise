Coffee Recipe


Ingredients:

  • Coffee powder
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
Directions:
  1. Mix 1 tsp of instant coffee per cup of hot water or hot water and milk solution.
  2. Serve hot with desired amount of sugar.
